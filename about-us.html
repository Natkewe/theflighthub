About Us

We're the travel-obsessed Travel Experts. We're here to open up the world for those who want to see it. Every day, we give people all around the world the opportunity to experience something amazing—travel! We love what we do! Whether its helping to inspire you to find your next vacation, or making your booking happen, we are here to help. We're the flagship brand of The Flight Hub, a UK Travel company. It's recognised as one of the top five best travel agencies in the UK today.

First opened in 2012 with a couple of great friends who just wanted to help others find their dream holidays, we are now a big company of more than 50+ employees over 2 stores. Despite our multible premises and busy stores, we have not lost any of the passion we first had, and our experiences passionate staff share the same interests and passions as us. Our stores specialize in flights and helping you to search for the best flights and venues to suit your needs, and our staff will always make sure they have plenty of time to sit and talk to you to help you find your dream location to holiday!

Our flights are made through SkyScanner who offer the best prices and the most convenient times and airport destinations. With this in mind, we help you get the best package of flights and helpful information on what you can do while at your destination. We have been helping people travel and fly for many years now, and this has helped us to offer and tailor the best service and customer experience every time. We are proud of our stores and the thousands of happy travelers we have helped create ever lasting memories for. We look forward to seeing you soon.
